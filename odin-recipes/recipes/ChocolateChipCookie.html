Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

A chocolate chip cookie is a sweet baked treat that is recognized by its butter flavor and the inclusion of chocolate chips. Some variations can include nuts, oatmeal or raisins as well.

Ingredients

1 1/2 c all-purpose flour, 1 egg, 1/2 tsp salt, 1 tsp vanilla, 1/2 tsp baking soda, 1/4 c brown sugar, 1/2 c sugar, 1 c chocolate chips, and 8 tbsp unsalted butter

    Step 1

    Using your electric mixer, combine both of the sugars and the butter.

    Step 2

    After the sugars and butter are mixed completely, turn the mixer on low and add the vanilla.

    Step 3

    Add the egg into the mixture and mix until it's just combined, but don't over mix this step.

    Step 4

    Now add the flour, baking soda and salt, mix until it has a crumbly texture.

    Step 5

    Add the chocolate chips and stir into the batter.

    Step 6

    Put into the refrigerator for an hour to chill.

    Step 7

    Roll 1 tbsp of batter into a ball and place on a baking sheet.

    Step 8

    Bake in an oven that's been preheated to 350Â° for 9-11 minutes until the bottoms are golden brown.

    Step 9

    Remove from the oven and place on a wire rack to cool.
