M&M Cookies

Description

The best M&M Cookies recipe is a classic soft and chewy cookie that deserves a place in every recipe box. It's a favorite at every party!

Ingredients

1 cup shortening, 1 cup packed brown sugar, ½ cup white sugar, 2 eggs, 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract, 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon baking soda, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 ½ cups candy-coated milk chocolate pieces (such as M&M's®)

    Step 1

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

    Step 2

    Beat shortening, brown sugar, white sugar, eggs, and vanilla in a mixing bowl until light and fluffy, 2 to 3 minutes.

    Step 3

    Add flour, baking soda, and salt; blend well. Stir in 1/2 of the chocolate candies.

    Step 4

    Drop dough by teaspoonfuls onto an ungreased baking sheet. Use remaining candies to decorate dough balls; press down slightly.

    Step 5

    Bake in batches in the preheated oven until lightly browned with soft centers, 9 to 11 minutes.

    Step 6

    Enjoy!
