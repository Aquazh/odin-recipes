Cookie recipes!

  • Classics!

    • You can’t go wrong with the classic chocolate chip cookie. This recipe includes melted butter, more brown sugar than white sugar, and an extra egg yolk for a chewy texture and big chocolate chunks for indulgent flavor.

  • Something healthier maybe?

    • These cookies feature a soft, cake-like texture, a slightly spiced flavor, and plenty of tangy cream cheese frosting on top. They’re just like bite-size pieces of carrot cake, with shredded carrot, walnuts, oats, and more.

  • Something more childish?

    • Loaded with M&Ms, these cookies elevate a regular chocolate chip cookie to a new level. They have crisp chewy edges, soft centers, and enough chocolatey goodness to go around.

  • More colorful?

    • Red velvet is delicious as a cake, but it’s even better as a cookie. These cookies feature that can’t-quite-put-a-finger-on-it taste of red velvet paired with creamy white chocolate chips
